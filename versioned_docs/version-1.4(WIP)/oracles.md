## Band

[Official documentation](https://docs.bandchain.org/band-standard-dataset/supported-blockchains.html)

Update frequency: updated every 1 hour with 1% deviation for stable coin / 5% deviation for non-stable coin.

Supported assets:
1. ADA/USD
2. BAND/USD
3. BAT/USD
4. BNB/USD
5. BTC/USD
6. BUSD/USD
7. CKB/USD
8. DAI/USD
9. ETH/USD
10. MKR/USD
11. SUSHI/USD
12. TUSD/USD
13. UNI/USD
14. USDC/USD
15. USDT/USD
16. YFI/USD
